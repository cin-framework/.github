CIN FRAMEWORK Reporting Guidelines

Reporting Guidelines

Introduction

This page serves as the official reference for reporting any unacceptable behavior or violations of community rules within the CIN Framework community. These guidelines are directly linked to our Code of Conduct and provide a clear pathway for addressing concerns.

Types of Issues You Can Report

You can report the following types of issues:

  • Verbal abuse or harassment
  • Posting inappropriate content or content outside the community scope
  • Behaviors that hinder development or collaboration
  • Violations of security or privacy policies

Official Reporting Channels

You can report issues through the following official channels:

Primary Email

support@cin-framework.com

Sensitive Cases Email

admin@cin-framework.com

Required Report Content

When submitting a report, please include the following information:

  • Name or identity of the person or entity (if available)
  • Clear and detailed description of the incident
  • Date, time, and location (or link to the incident if online)
  • Any evidence (images, links, messages)

Confidentiality and Reporter Protection

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of the reporter's identity. Any report is handled with complete confidentiality, and the reporter's identity will not be disclosed without their consent or unless required by law.

Processing Mechanism

Our report processing follows these steps:

1. Acknowledgment

Report receipt confirmed within 48 hours

2. Review

Report reviewed by Governing Body

3. Action

Appropriate action taken (warning, ban, other measures)

Report Retention Period

Report records are retained for 12 months before being permanently deleted, unless extended retention is required for ongoing investigations or legal requirements.

Page Updates

These guidelines may be updated at any time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes.
